A Pie to Die For

Have you ever craved a slice of pie but the meer thought of store bought made you gag?

Well fear not! This recipe will have you searching Amazon for one of those big funny bakers hats in just 5 minutes! Yes 5 minutes.

Get up out of your recliner, put some slippers on and go grab these things from the store.

Innards

  • 9 inch deep dish pie crust, frozen
  • 1 can (15 ounce) can pumpkin
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 eggs, just buy a dozen for home, maybe you'll eat them
  • 1 tablespoon McCormick Pumkin Pie Spice
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla whipped cream
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Place pie crust on large foil-lined baking sheet.
  2. Mix pumpkin, milk, eggs and pumpkin pie spice in large bowl until smooth. Pour into crust.
  3. Bake 15 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F. Bake 40 minutes longer or until knife inserted 1 inch from crust comes out clean. Cool completely on wire rack. Serve with Vanilla Whipped Cream, if desired.
